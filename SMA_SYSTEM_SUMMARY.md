### Appointment Booking
```python

    slot_id=slot_uuid,

    hold_duration_minutes=10
)

# Step 2: Confirm appointment
confirm_request = AppointmentConfirmRequest(
    patient_id=patient_uuid,
    hold_token=hold_result['hold_token'],
```
